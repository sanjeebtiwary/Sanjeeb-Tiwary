Queue Waiting
Time Prediction

This research delves into the realm of predicting waiting times in real-life banking queues, a ubiquitous aspect of daily life. Accurate waiting time prediction is essential for efficient queue management, and this paper explores various machine learning algorithms to achieve this goal. Our findings demonstrate the efficacy of SVR in predicting waiting times for everyone in the queue, making it a viable and robust solution for queue analysis in the context of developing countries' banking systems.
